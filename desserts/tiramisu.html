Tiramisu - Italian speciality

By: Anna

Tiramisu is traditionally made of mascarpone cheese and Savoiardi biscuits. Making Tiramisu is not difficult at all.

Ingredients:

  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 tbs brown sugar
  • 2,5 dl mascarpone cheese
  • 1,5 dl cold espresso
  • 12-15 savoiardi biscuits
  • vanilla seeds
  • cocoa powder
  • grted dark chocolate

Instructions:

  1. Stir together the egg yolks and sugar until it has a creamy consistency.
  2. Add the vanilla seeds and the mascarpone cheese.
  3. Dip biscuits into the cold espresso. Be careful not to soak them for too long
  4. Place some of the biscuits on a plate. Then put a layer of cheese mixture on top. Continue with another layer of biscuits and finish with the rest of the cheese mixture. Finish by sifting over a layer of cocoa powder.
  5. Leave the dessert in the fridge for 3-4 hours or overnight
  6. Just before serving, grate some chocolate and sprinkle over the dessert.

    7. Enjoy with a good cup of coffee!
