Apple pie

By: Henrik

This classic apple pie is made with a buttery and flaky crust, filled with sweet and tart spiced apples, and topped with a beautiful lattice crust and brushed with egg for a shiny finish.

Ingredients:

  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 150g unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 4 tbsp cold water
  • 6 large apples, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 50g granulated sugar
  • 50g light brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar. Add the cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your hands to mix until the mixture resembles coarse sand.
  2. Add the cold water and mix until the dough comes together in a ball. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  4. In another mixing bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Mix well.
  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the chilled dough to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Place the dough into the pie dish, trimming any excess dough from the edges.
  6. Pour the apple mixture into the pie dish. Dot the top of the apples with small pieces of unsalted butter.
  7. Roll out the remaining dough to create a lattice pattern on top of the pie. Brush the beaten egg over the top of the lattice.
  8. Bake the pie for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  9. Let the pie cool for at least 30 minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your delicious apple pie!
