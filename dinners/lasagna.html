Traditional Beef Lasagna

By: Henrik

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide pasta sheets, tomato sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, savory ground beef, and melted mozzarella cheese, baked to perfection in the oven.

Ingredients:

  • 400g lasagna sheets
  • 400g beef mince
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans of chopped tomatoes(400g each)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 500g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 100g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 500g mozzarella cheese, grated
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets in boiling salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large saucepan, brown the beef mince over medium-high heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until the onion is translucent.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, and dried basil to the saucepan. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, and parmesan cheese.
  6. In a 20cm x 30cm baking dish, spread a thin layer of the beef and tomato sauce on the bottom. Cover with a layer of lasagna sheets, followed by a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture, and a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat this layering process until all ingredients are used up.
  7. Top with a final layer of mozzarella cheese and sprinkle with a little extra parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let the lasagna cool for 5-10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.

Enjoy!
